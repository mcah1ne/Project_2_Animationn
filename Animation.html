Story

This puzzle has a back story! The square is trying to get to the circle, but the circle is trying to get to the square to tell it that it is a ghost and can go through walls. When the circle got to where the square was it noticed that the square has left so it started to jump because it was very upset. The same story happened to the square and they lived to never see each other again. This story is not the happiest, but is the truth in which they live their lives and told the story to me.
